Hi. I'm Sherri.

I am an Instructional Designer who is passionate about visual storytelling and creating engaging learning experiences that solve real-world problems. When I'm not lost in the rabbit hole of books and blogs, you can find me hiking, photographing Hawaii's natural beauty, and baking.

My first professional area of interest is Social and Emotinal Learning. I'm interested in this because providing youth with opportunities to develop social and emotional competencies has been shown to decrease problem behaviors and increase school connectedness,achievement, attendance, and positive social behaviors.

My second area of interest is Learning Experience Design From the beginning of the design process, I aim to keep the learner and their needs in mind to create the best possible experience for users.

My third area of interest is interactivity and multimedia environments. I want to harness the power of technology to strengthen individuals and teams.
